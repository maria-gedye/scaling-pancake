Original recipe
3 eggs
1/2 c sugar
2 c milk
1 c flour
1 t baking powder
2 T maple syrup
2 handfuls of chocolate chips

DIRECTIONS
1. Sift dry ingredients together in bowl
2. Warm milk and syrup in microwave to room temperature and add to dry ingredients
3. Mix until just combined
4. Add in chocolate chips
5. Using a medium heat, add butter to non stick pan
6. Fill pan with batter and cook for 3 min or until you see bubbles
7. Flip over and cook for further 2 min

Makes 10 large pancakes
